Yet Another Multipurpose Bot!

This is a multipurpose discord bot, which has many categories like Economy, Levelling and many more. It also has many different games you can play in-discord!

Some Features:

Economy

YAMBot has a built-in economy system, which allows you to earn coins, and use them to buy various items from the shop. You can also deposit and withdraw coins from the bank too!

WIP

Leveling

YAMBot has a built-in levelling system, which allows you to gain XP while chatting and get awesome level-up rewards!

Auto Moderation

YAMBot has a built-in auto-moderation system, which includes Anti-Spam, Anti-Raid, Banned words and much more!
